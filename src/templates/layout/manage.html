Creating New Content

To create new content hover over one of the content types on the navigation bar at the top of this page and click on the "Create" link.

A form will appear in the main window. Add your desired content into the form fields and click the "Save" button.

Some content types require certain form fields to be filled out completely or to be formated in a particular way. If information is missing or incorrectly formatted you will be prompted by an error message. Content will not be saved until the errors have been resolved.

Editing Content

To edit existing content, hover over a content drop down and click on the title of the item you sould like to edit.

Make the desired changes and click the "Save" button. Changes will not be saved until "Save" is clicked and you recieve the message stating that the document has been saved.

Deleting Content

To delete a content, hover over a content drop down and click on the title of the item you wish to delete. Click the "Delete" button next to the save button and confirm that you want to delete the item.

Deleted items cannot be recovered after you confirm the deletion.
